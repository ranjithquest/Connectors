Open issues

BlockerAuthentication refused

Microsoft attempted to sign in using Basic Auth with svc-copilot@contoso.com. ServiceNow returned 403 — the account may have MFA enabled or the password was recently rotated.

Blocker
Disable MFA for this service account in ServiceNow, or switch the connector authentication to OAuth 2.0.
BlockerService account missing read role

The service account svc-copilot@contoso.com does not have the snc_read role in ServiceNow. Without it, Microsoft cannot index any knowledge articles.

Blocker
In ServiceNow go to User Administration → Users → open the service account → assign the snc_read role.
WorkaroundCustom ACL scripts partially ignored

Your ServiceNow instance uses custom ACL scripts to control article visibility. Microsoft's connector only honours role-based access criteria — scripted ACLs are bypassed, which may expose restricted articles in Copilot results.

Workaround
WorkaroundSync scope too broad

The connector is indexing all knowledge bases including HR Confidential and Legal Review. These may contain content not intended for Copilot search results.

Workaround
Go to the Sync tab in connector settings and restrict indexing to specific approved knowledge bases only.
BlockerACL sync failed — zero search results

The connector completed a full sync and indexed content, but ACL sync silently failed. All users are seeing zero search results in Copilot because no permissions were propagated. This issue can go undetected for days without an explicit health indicator.

Blocker
In ServiceNow, go to User Administration → Users → open the service account → verify that both acl_read and glide_read roles are assigned. Then trigger a manual ACL sync from the connector settings.
How toSwitch to OAuth 2.0

Basic Auth is currently working but OAuth 2.0 is more secure for long-running connectors. It avoids sync failures when service account passwords are rotated.

How to
In connector setup, go to the Setup tab → Authentication type → select OAuth 2.0 and provide a client ID and secret from ServiceNow.
How toIncrease sync frequency

The connector is set to sync weekly but 47 new HR policy articles were added in the last 24 hours. Search results in Copilot may lag behind by up to 7 days.

How to
In connector settings, go to the Sync tab → Incremental sync and change the schedule to daily.
